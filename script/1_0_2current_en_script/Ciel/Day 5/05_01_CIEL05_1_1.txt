I can't abandon her.
She's going to get her blood sucked by that vampire. Imagining this gets my nerves quivering. I can't allow that. I may have lost my mind, but that much, I can't stand. //神経を加速させる lit. quickens my nerves, I see nerves quivering, or nerves straining
<Page2>
I propel my legs instinctively.
I've stopped superfluous things like breathing. 
Right beside me, a fingertip slightly grazes me so as to stop me, but that wasn't enough. After all, I moved before my thoughts could catch up. 
<Page3>
However, I was too far.
My human legs were too slow. 
The open mouth of the vampire was like that of a carnivorous plant.
He was like a giant sticky leech biting from the collarbone to the neck of the woman.

"Ahhhh-----aa! Aaaaaa, aaa------ ahhhh...!"
The woman's body convulses.
The sound of a life being wrung out.
Gulp gulp gulp.
The foul sound of him swallowing resonates in my ears and in my brain.

――― That's when my consciousness broke.
――― This is the second time I see right in front of my eyes someone handled like food.
